About Us

As a part of Technology advancement in the field of health sector, Our Website aims to Introduce QR based Health cards to enhance users Healthcare experience.


The data on these cards will not be accessible unless the card is read by a QR Code reader on a mobile phone or desktop and verified by a secure OTP. When read, the provider will be able to access the patient’s medical records and the patients can update the card with any relevant changes.
