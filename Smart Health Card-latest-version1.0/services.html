Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Health Card ?
Every patient who wishes to store their health records digitally must start by generating a Health Card. Each Health Card will be linked to the patient’s profile page of our website which will be used after the patient's consent by providing an OTP sent to their phone number and then allow for seamless flow of health information from the Health Records module.
Is my Health ID unique?
Your Health Card is generated by using your basic details and Mobile Number. Thus, it will be unique to you and you will have all your health records linked to this ID.
How do I generate a Health Card?
First, you need to sign up by creating an account in our website. Then there will be one tab ‘Generate Health Card’ . Click on that. Fill in your name and upload your photo which you want on your Health Card. Then click on Generate and your Health Card is successfully generated.
What do I do if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, click on ‘Forgot your password?’ then enter your registered email id and you will be sent a email to change your password.
What all do I need to register for a Health Card?
Name, Date of Birth, Gender and Mobile No.
How to change my password ?
You can change your password from the profile section by entering your current password then the new password.
